ABOUT US

CPI is a Nonprofit Organization for Peace in Cameroon.

Registered in the Commonwealth of Virginia, U.S.A., CPI was granted a 501 (c) 3 status by the Internal Revenue Service. CPI abides to all applicable regulations and guidelines pertaining to organization under 501 (c) 3. CPI may organize fundraising events and receive donations.
